Arizona

Arizona is where I call home. Once a Mexican territory, Arizona was founded in 1912. Arizona or the Grand Canyon State is in the southwest of the United States. It is known for it's hot desert climate, major sports teams, a lot of cactus, and colorful sunsets. Arizona is home to over 15 national parks and monuments including possibly the most famous and reason for the Arizona's nickmame, the Grand Canyon. The Grand Canyon is a expansive canyon carved over billions of years by the Colorado River. The Grand Canyon is 277 miles long, 18 miles across at it's widest location, and 1 mile deep. In 1903, President Theodore Roosevelt said "The Grand Canyon filles me with awe. It is beyond comparison- beyond description; absolutely unparalleled throughout the wide world... Let this great wonder of nature remain at it is now. Do nothing to mar its grandeur, sublimity and loveliness." Later, in 1908, President Roosevent declared the Grand Canyon a National Monument. -nps.gov/grca/index.htm-
